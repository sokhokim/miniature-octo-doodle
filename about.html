About

Warm, goal-oriented therapy that helps people heal and grow.

Rest & Restore Therapy is rooted in reliability, authenticity, and compassion. Linda Kim describes her style as warm, light-hearted, and goal-oriented, with undivided attention, genuine care, and practical new skills for clients. A faith-based Christian perspective is available for clients who request it, while therapy is provided to people of all backgrounds.

Approach

Sessions often blend interventions from CBT, ACT, motivational interviewing, mindfulness-based strategies, somatic awareness, narrative approaches, and other integrative tools depending on the client’s goals. Linda also describes her typical session structure as connect, reflect, and direct.

  • Connect: begin with a check-in and grounding when needed.
  • Reflect: explore what has happened and what it means.
  • Direct: build insight, coping tools, and agreed next steps.

Training & background

Linda is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker in Maryland, North Carolina, and Washington. Publicly listed trainings include TF-CBT, EMDR through an EMDRIA program, ACT, ASIST for suicide intervention, CBT for anxiety/phobias/depression, and Motivational Interviewing.

Her background includes work with children through adults with PTSD, anxiety, phobias, and mood disorders, including prior roles in family trauma therapy, outpatient therapy, and crisis counseling.
