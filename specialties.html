Specialties

Support for trauma, anxiety, and depression.

The current website highlights three core specialty areas, each with targeted interventions and populations served.

Trauma

Main interventions listed: EMDR, TF-CBT, and narrative therapy.

  • PTSD
  • Acute Stress Disorder
  • Adjustment Disorder with anxiety or depression
  • Childhood trauma
  • Attachment trauma
  • Medical trauma

Anxiety

Main interventions listed: CBT, exposure, mindfulness-based, and somatic awareness approaches.

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder
  • Social Anxiety Disorder
  • Panic attacks
  • Separation anxiety
  • Specific phobias
  • Stress from life transitions

Depression

Main interventions listed: CBT, motivational interviewing, solution-focused therapy, narrative therapy, and ACT.

  • Major Depressive Disorder
  • Postpartum depression
  • Depressed feelings tied to hormonal changes
  • Self-esteem issues
  • Isolation and guilt
